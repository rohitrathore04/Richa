About Hotel Richa

A Home Away From Home in the Holy City

Hotel Richa is a comfortable and family-friendly hotel in Ujjain, offering a perfect blend of hospitality, convenience, and modern amenities. Located on Sanwer Road with easy access to the city's major attractions, the hotel provides well-maintained rooms, delicious food, and personalized service for pilgrims, tourists, families, and business travelers.

Whether you are visiting Ujjain for spiritual purposes or a relaxing stay, Hotel Richa ensures a pleasant and memorable experience.
